The Venue

Hammond Castle Museum

A stunning 1920s seaside castle and museum with sweeping Atlantic views, stone archways, and the dramatic Great Hall—perfect for a fairytale “I do.”

Open in Google Maps Open in Apple Maps

Parking: On-site and free but limited. Please arrive early; rideshare is encouraged.

Accessibility: Historic property with many stairs; unfortunately not wheelchair accessible.

Travel

Getting There

Fly into Boston Logan International Airport (BOS). The castle is roughly 27 miles north on MA-128 along Cape Ann (about 45–50 minutes by car depending on traffic). Consider rideshare or rental cars for easiest access.

  • Driving: I-95 N → MA-128 N to Gloucester; follow signs for Route 127 and Hesperus Ave.
  • Rail: MBTA Commuter Rail Rockport Line to Gloucester, then a short taxi/rideshare to the venue.

Local Tips

  • Weekend coastal traffic can be heavy—pad your schedule.
  • There are stairs from the parking lot down to the castle entrance.
  • Plan your return trip if using rideshare later in the evening.
